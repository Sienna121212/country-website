High Tatras

As home of the tallest mountains in the country, the High Tatras makes for some pretty unforgettable
hiking and rock climbing. while in winter, there are loads of great slopes and pistes to ski, sled, and
snowboard down. The high majestic mountain range really is a sight to see; its jagged rocky peaks exceeding 2,500 m above
sea level, and sparkling alpine lakes and meadows can be found tucked away among its many slopes and valleys.
The High Tatras are almost a national symbol! and the snow-capped Mount Krivan is the most distinctive peak.
Because of the breathtakingly beautiful landscapes, the mountain range positions itself perfectly to all different
types of outdoor activities. There are plenty of paths and trails for you to hike, cycle, or ski along, and the views
are beautiful and breathtaking!
