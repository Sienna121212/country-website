Orava Castle

Sitting upon a hilltop amid a large green forest stretching down its steep slopes,
Orava Castle most definitaly looks impressive, as vast green hills lie off in the far
distance, and the Orava River runs right below the castles cliff face.
One of the most beautiful castles in the whole of Slovakia, Orava Castle was built all the way
back in the 13th century
As each generation added its own touches and additions, the castle shows off a variety of architectural styles
with Romanesque, Gothic, and Renaissance features all as wonderful examples. Its museum has some old
artifacts and archaeological findings that document its rich and facinating history. Throughout the many many
