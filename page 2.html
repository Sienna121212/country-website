Banska Stiavnica

Lying at the center of what used to be an enormous caldera, Banská Štiavnica
is a fascinating city to explore! Much of its glorious wealth is from the rich
mineral deposits that were once found in the ground around the town a long time ago.
what used to be a mining town, this city has a magnificent historic center, with lots
of beautiful churches, palaces, and museums!
